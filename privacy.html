Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

DoseMap is designed as a local-first tracker for GLP-1 and peptide-related routines. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information Stored On Your Device

DoseMap may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Medication or compound names you enter.
  • Vial strength, water amount, calculated units, and doses left.
  • Dose logs, dates, injection sites, notes, schedules, and reminders.
  • Symptoms, daily wins, onboarding answers, and app preferences.
  • Local account details, such as an email address or Apple sign-in identifier, if you choose to add them.

Local Storage

Your tracker history is stored on your device. DoseMap does not operate a medical records backend for this app. If you delete the app or reset local data, local tracker data may be removed from the device.

Subscriptions And Purchases

DoseMap may use Apple in-app purchases and RevenueCat to manage subscriptions, purchase status, entitlements, and restore-purchase flows. Apple and RevenueCat may process purchase-related identifiers and transaction information according to their own policies.

Notifications

If you allow reminders, DoseMap uses local notifications on your device. Notifications are intended only as organizational reminders and are not medical instructions.

Medical Privacy Notice

DoseMap is not a medical provider, pharmacy, emergency service, or clinical record system. Do not use the app as your only source for medical decisions.

Sharing And Export

If you export or share your tracker information, you choose where that exported information goes. Be careful when sharing health-adjacent information with other apps, services, or people.

Account Deletion And Data Reset

The app includes local account deletion and reset flows. These remove local account details and tracker data from the device where available. Deleting local data does not cancel an Apple subscription. Subscriptions must be managed through your Apple ID settings.

Children

DoseMap is not intended for children. Do not use the app if you are not old enough to manage health-related app usage under the laws that apply to you.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, email supportdosemap@gmail.com.
